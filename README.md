# 🏺 BHK Employee App

## 📌 Overview

**BHK Employee App** is a digital platform designed to empower **artisans (craft workers)** by helping them manage their work, products, and employees efficiently.

It also enables **collaboration between different artisans**, allowing them to complete products together and increase their productivity and sales.

---

## 🎯 Purpose

* Digitize traditional artisan work
* Simplify product showcasing and selling
* Enable teamwork between artisans
* Help artisans who are not tech-savvy

---

## 🔑 Key Features

### 👥 Employee Management

* Add and manage employees
* Assign work easily

### 🛍️ Product Management

* Add, update, and showcase products
* Maintain product listings

### 📦 Order Flow & Collaboration

* Manage incoming orders
* Forward work to other artisans

### 🔗 Artisan Collaboration (Core Feature)

Example:

* One artisan creates **clay pots (Matka)**
* Another artisan specializes in **painting**
* Orders can be passed between artisans
* Final product is completed collaboratively

---

## 💡 Use Case

If a pot maker receives an order:

1. Creates the base product
2. Assigns it to a painter artisan
3. Painter completes finishing
4. Final product is delivered

---

## 🚀 Benefits

* Supports local artisans
* Increases productivity
* Enables teamwork
* Expands business opportunities

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

* Flutter
* GetX (State Management)
* REST API Integration
